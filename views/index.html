FLICKERS alpha

This is an R & D project to find the best way of presenting Rachel Henson's flick-book portfolio on the internet.

Choose an item from the menu to navigate to the flick-book viewer. When the images have loaded, use the slider to flick through the pages.

WARNING: this application is image heavy!
You may wish to connect wirelessly first.
